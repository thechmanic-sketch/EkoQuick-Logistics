Booking

How do I book a delivery?

Sign up, enter your pickup and delivery details, choose a vehicle class, get a quote, and confirm — the whole flow takes under a minute.

Can I book for someone else to receive it?

Yes — enter the recipient's name, phone, and address in the booking wizard; they don't need an Ekoquick account.

Can I schedule a delivery for later?

Yes — choose "Schedule for Later" at the vehicle step and pick a date and time.

What is "Request a Pickup"?

A separate flow for when you've already bought something in-store and just need a driver to collect it and bring it to you — no warehouse in between.

Pricing

How is my delivery price calculated?

Base fare for your chosen vehicle class, plus a rate per kilometre for the real driving distance. See our pricing page for the full breakdown and a live estimator.

Are there hidden fees?

No. Express adds a fixed 50%, and waiting time only applies if your driver is kept waiting beyond the free window — both shown before you confirm.

Tracking

How do I track my delivery?

While logged in, your dashboard shows live status and map tracking. You can also use our public Track Parcel page with your tracking number and phone number, no login required.

What is the pickup/delivery code?

Two short codes generated at booking — your driver must be given the pickup code before collecting, and the delivery code before the job is marked complete, confirming the right parcel changed hands.

Drivers

Are Ekoquick drivers vetted?

Yes — every driver's ID, licence, and vehicle documents are reviewed and approved by our team before they can take a job.

How do I become a driver?

See our driver requirements page and sign up — set your own hours and get paid per job.

Payments

How do I pay for a delivery?

Cash on delivery is available now. Card payments are coming soon; EFT is available for approved business accounts with manual proof-of-payment verification.

Who do I pay — Ekoquick or the driver directly?

Cash payments go directly to your driver at collection or delivery, as agreed at booking.

Cancellations

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes, from your dashboard while the job hasn't yet been picked up. Once a driver has collected your parcel, contact support instead.

Does cancelling cost anything?

Cancelling before a driver is dispatched is free. If a driver has already been assigned and is en route, a partial fee may apply — shown before you confirm the cancellation.

Refunds

How do refunds work?

If a job is cancelled or something goes wrong on our end, refunds are reviewed and processed by our admin team — contact support with your job details to start one.

How long do refunds take?

Once approved, refunds are processed as quickly as your original payment method allows — cash refunds are typically resolved directly with support.

Support

How do I contact support?

WhatsApp us directly, or use the in-app support ticket system from your dashboard once logged in. See our contact page for all options.

What if my driver isn't responding?

Use the in-app chat to reach your driver first; if there's no response, contact support and we'll step in directly.

Business Deliveries

Do you offer business accounts?

Yes — volume-based rates, priority dispatch, and EFT payment options. See our business page to get started.

Can I set up recurring deliveries?

Yes — tell us your regular routes and schedule and we'll quote a business rate based on volume and frequency.

The terms governing your use of Ekoquick as a customer or driver, under the laws of South Africa.

This document is a template starting point covering the areas below. We recommend having it reviewed by a South African attorney before relying on it as your final legal document.

1.Definitions

"Platform" means the Ekoquick website and applications. "Customer" means a person booking a delivery. "Driver" means an independent driver accepting jobs through the Platform. "Job" means a single booked delivery. "We"/"us"/"Ekoquick" refers to the operator of the Platform.

2.Acceptance of Terms

By creating an account or using the Platform, you agree to these Terms & Conditions and our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the Platform.

3.Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and legally capable of entering into a binding agreement to use the Platform. Drivers must additionally hold a valid South African driver's licence, vehicle registration, and insurance appropriate to their vehicle class.

4.Bookings

Customers book deliveries by providing pickup and drop-off details, package information, and selecting a vehicle class. A quote is generated based on distance and vehicle class before you confirm. Bookings are subject to driver availability.

5.Payments

Payment is due as agreed at booking — currently cash on delivery, with EFT available to approved business accounts (verified manually) and card payments to follow once our payment gateway is connected. Ekoquick retains a commission on each completed job; drivers receive the remainder.

6.Cancellations

Customers may cancel a job before a driver is dispatched at no charge. Once a driver has been assigned and is en route, a cancellation fee may apply, shown before you confirm the cancellation. Repeated cancellations may affect your ability to book future deliveries.

7.Driver Responsibilities

  • Hold a valid licence, registration, and insurance for the vehicle class used.
  • Collect and deliver parcels using the pickup/delivery codes to confirm handoff.
  • Handle parcels marked fragile, keep-upright, or handle-with-care accordingly.
  • Communicate professionally with customers via the in-app chat.
  • Comply with all road traffic laws at all times while performing a job.

8.Customer Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate pickup/drop-off addresses and contact details.
  • Accurately describe the parcel, including weight, size, and any special handling needs.
  • Not book prohibited items (see below).
  • Be reasonably available or provide a contact person at pickup and drop-off.
  • Pay the agreed fare through the agreed payment method.

9.Prohibited Items

The Platform may not be used to send illegal goods, weapons, hazardous or flammable materials, live animals, human remains, cash or currency, or any item prohibited under South African law. Ekoquick reserves the right to refuse or cancel any job suspected of violating this clause.

10.Proof of Delivery

Each job generates a pickup code and a delivery code. The driver must be given the pickup code before collecting the parcel and the delivery code before the job is marked complete — this is the Platform's confirmation that the correct parcel changed hands at each end.

11.Tracking

Customers can track an active delivery live via their dashboard or the public Track Parcel page, using their tracking number and the phone number on the job.

12.Refunds

Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis where a job was cancelled by Ekoquick or a driver, or where service was not delivered as described. Refund requests should be submitted via our contact page or in-app support with the relevant job details.

13.Liability

Ekoquick facilitates connections between customers and independent drivers and is not itself the carrier. To the extent permitted by South African law, our liability for any loss or damage is limited to the value of the affected job's delivery fee, except where liability cannot lawfully be excluded or limited.

14.Insurance

Drivers are responsible for maintaining their own vehicle insurance appropriate to commercial or courier use where required by law. Ekoquick does not currently provide parcel insurance; customers sending high-value items should arrange their own cover.

15.Privacy

16.Termination

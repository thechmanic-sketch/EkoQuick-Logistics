Start earning.

Bring your own motorbike, bakkie or one-tonne truck, work on your own schedule, and get paid per completed job — anywhere across KwaZulu-Natal.

How dispatch works

Once you're signed up and approved, your account goes onto the active driver roster. When a customer books a job that matches your vehicle class and area, our dispatch team assigns it directly to you — no bidding, no browsing a job board, no waiting around refreshing a screen.

You accept the job from your driver dashboard, head to the pickup point, and turn on live tracking so the customer can follow your route until drop-off.

What you need

  • DOC/01
    Valid Driving License Front and back side, current and unexpired.
  • DOC/02
    Government ID Proof South African ID or passport.
  • DOC/03
    Vehicle Registration Certificate Registration document (RC) for the vehicle you'll be driving.
  • DOC/04
    Vehicle Insurance Valid, current insurance document.
  • DOC/05
    Profile Photo A clear, recent photograph for your driver profile.

All documents must be clear and valid — incorrect or incomplete information may lead to your application being rejected.

FAQ

Can I combine services, like a scheduled express delivery?

Yes — vehicle class, delivery type (standard/express), and schedule (now/later) are all chosen independently when you book, so you can mix them however the job needs.

What happens if my parcel is fragile or needs to stay upright?

Mark it at booking — fragile, keep upright, and handle-with-care are all flags your driver sees before they collect, alongside your package description.

Is there a weight limit I can't go over at all?

The limits above are guidelines per vehicle class. For anything heavier or bulkier, choose a larger vehicle class (bakkie or truck) at booking — the quote adjusts automatically.

Do you deliver outside KwaZulu-Natal?

Not yet — Ekoquick operates within KwaZulu-Natal only for now. See our coverage page for the full list of towns.

How is the price for my delivery calculated?

Base fare for your chosen vehicle class, plus a rate per kilometre for the real driving distance between your two addresses. See the full breakdown on our pricing page.

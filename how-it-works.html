Manifest

How a job moves

Four line items, start to finish. No call centre, no dispatcher on hold — just a route, a tariff and a driver already on the road.

STEP/01 — Enter the route

Type in your pickup address and your destination — anywhere within our KwaZulu-Natal coverage area. Ekoquick works out the real driving distance between the two points and holds it against the job.

There's no need to guess at a price or call ahead — the route is calculated the moment you enter both addresses, before you commit to anything.

STEP/02 — Read the tariff

Choose your vehicle class — motorbike, bakkie or one-tonne truck — and see the full fare on screen: base fee plus the per-kilometre rate for the distance calculated in step one. What you see before confirming is what you pay; there's no metered surcharge added after the fact.

Worked example

Durban to Pinetown (22km) on a Motor Bike: R45 base + (22km × R6.50) = R188.00

See the full tariff sheet for rates across all three vehicle classes.

STEP/03 — Driver dispatched

Once you confirm the job, it's placed on the active job board. Our dispatch team assigns the nearest available driver, who accepts and heads straight to your pickup point. You'll see the assigned driver's details as soon as the job is picked up.

STEP/04 — Tracked live

From collection to drop-off, you can watch your driver move in real time on a live map — powered by the driver's own GPS position, updated continuously for the length of the trip. No refreshing, no guessing an ETA.

When the parcel is handed over, the job is marked complete and you're asked to rate the driver — helping keep service quality high across the fleet.

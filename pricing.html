Tariff sheet

Priced by the kilometre

Every fare is base fee plus distance, worked out from the real road distance between your two addresses. No surge pricing, no surprise add-ons at the door.

The formula

Every job is priced the same way, regardless of vehicle class:

Fare formula

Total fare = Base fare + (Rate per km × Distance in km)

The base fare covers collection and handling. The per-kilometre rate covers the trip itself. Both are fixed per vehicle class — see the full sheet below.

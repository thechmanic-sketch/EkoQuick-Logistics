FAQ

Is the estimate above the exact price I'll pay?

It's very close — it uses the same formula and real road distance as the actual booking flow. The final quote is confirmed in your dashboard once you enter both addresses at booking time.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. Base fare plus distance is the whole formula. Express adds a fixed 50% for priority dispatch, and waiting time only applies if your driver is kept waiting beyond the free window — both shown clearly before you confirm.

How do I pay?

Cash on delivery is available now. Card payments are coming soon once our payment gateway is connected; EFT is available for approved business accounts with manual proof-of-payment verification.

Does the price change during peak hours?

The base formula doesn't change. What changes is driver availability — booking Express during busy periods gets your job dispatched ahead of standard jobs in the queue.

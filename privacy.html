Privacy Policy

How Ekoquick collects, uses, and protects your personal information, in line with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

Last Updated: 20 July 2026  · 

This policy is a template starting point covering the areas below. We recommend having it reviewed by a South African attorney familiar with POPIA before relying on it as your final legal document.

1.Introduction

Ekoquick ("we", "us", "our") operates a delivery platform connecting customers, drivers, and administrators across KwaZulu-Natal. This policy explains what personal information we collect through our website, apps, and services, why we collect it, and what rights you have over it.

2.Information We Collect

  • Account details: name, username, email, phone number, password (stored encrypted).
  • Delivery details: pickup and delivery addresses, recipient names and phone numbers, package descriptions.
  • Payment information: payment method chosen, proof-of-payment documents for EFT (we do not store card numbers).
  • Driver-specific information: vehicle details, licence, ID, and insurance documents for verification.
  • Location data: pickup/drop-off coordinates and, for drivers, live location while a delivery is active.
  • Communications: in-app chat messages, support tickets, and any contact form submissions.
  • Technical data: device/browser information collected automatically for security and troubleshooting.

3.How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and provide the delivery service you request.
  • To match customers with drivers, calculate quotes, and dispatch jobs.
  • To verify driver identity, vehicle, and documentation before approval.
  • To send notifications about your bookings, account, and — where you've opted in — promotions.
  • To investigate complaints, disputes, and fraud, and to keep the platform secure.
  • To comply with legal obligations, including tax and financial record-keeping.

4.Cookies

We use essential cookies/local storage to keep you logged in and remember your preferences (such as theme). We do not currently use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

5.Location Data

Pickup and drop-off coordinates are collected to calculate distance and route your delivery. Driver location is shared with the customer only for the duration of an active job, so both parties can track progress in real time.

6.Driver Information

Drivers provide ID, licence, vehicle registration, and insurance documents for verification before approval. These documents are stored securely and used only for verification, compliance, and dispute resolution — not shared with customers beyond the driver's name, vehicle, and rating.

7.Customer Information

Customer information (name, contact details, addresses) is shared with the assigned driver only as needed to complete a specific delivery, and is not shared with other customers or third parties for marketing purposes.

8.Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only: with the driver or customer involved in a specific job (as needed to complete it), with service providers who help us operate the platform (e.g. our database and hosting providers), or where required by law.

9.Data Security

Access to personal information is controlled through row-level security policies and role-based access (customer, driver, admin) on our database. Passwords are encrypted; sensitive documents are stored in access-controlled storage. No system is 100% secure, and we continually work to improve our safeguards.

10.Data Retention

We retain account and delivery records for as long as your account is active and as needed to meet legal, tax, and dispute-resolution obligations thereafter. You may request deletion of your account, subject to records we're legally required to keep.

11.Your Rights

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, such as promotional communications.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa if you believe your rights have been infringed.

12.POPIA Compliance

We process personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA), collecting only what is necessary for a specified purpose, with appropriate safeguards, and giving data subjects the rights set out above.

13.Children's Privacy

Ekoquick's services are not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without the consent of a parent or guardian, as required under POPIA.

14.Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above, and significant changes may be communicated via email or in-app notification.

15.Contact Information

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact us via our contact page or WhatsApp us directly.
